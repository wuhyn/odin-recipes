Carbonara

Description

This recipe describes the steps in making a one-pan rich and creamy carbonara with a combo of cheese, bacon and eggs.

Ingredients

  • 250g pappardelle 250 grams pappardelle
  • 3/4 cup cooking cream 3 quarters cup cooking cream
  • 100g parmesan, finely grated
  • 3 free range egg yolks
  • 250g middle bacon rashers, rind removed, roughly chopped
  • 2/3 cup frozen peas
  • 2 thyme sprigs, to serve

Steps

  1. Fill a large, deep frying pan with water until two-thirds full. Bring to the boil over medium-high heat.
  2. Add pasta and cook for 7 minutes or until tender. Drain, reserving 1/2 cup cooking liquid.
  3. Meanwhile, whisk cream, two-thirds of the parmesan and egg yolks in a small bowl.
  4. Heat oil in same frying pan over medium-high heat. Cook bacon for 5 minutes, stirring, or until browned.
  5. Add peas and cook for 1 minute or until peas are tender.
  6. Remove pan from heat.
  7. Add pasta, cream mixture and reserved cooking liquid, then toss until pasta is coated.
  8. Serve topped with remaining parmesan and thyme. Season with pepper.
