Apple Pie

Description

This recipe describes the steps in making an apple pie with gold brown crust and perfectly cooked apples.

Ingredients

  • 6 apples
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1 tbs sugar to taste
  • 2 cups self-raising flour
  • 125g butter
  • 2 tbs sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbs water

Steps

  1. Peel and slice apples into pot with sugar and water.
  2. Boil and simmer until cooked.
  3. Sift flour into mixing pot.
  4. Add sugar, butter and rub it together until the mixture resemble crumbles.
  5. Crack the eggs in a bowl and beat the eggs until yolk and egg white are combined.
  6. Add eggs and water to the mixture.
  7. Roll out half the mix with rolling pin and add flour as required.
  8. Cover bottom of greased and flour pie dish with rolled out pastry.
  9. Add apples and repeat with the rest of the pastry on top.
  10. Cut around edge of the pie dish with a knife.
  11. Press the layers together using a fork with flour on it.
  12. Brush with a little milk and sprinkle with caster sugar.
  13. Bake at 180C for roughly fro 45-60 minutes until golden brown.
