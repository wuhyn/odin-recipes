Butter Chicken

Description

This recipe describes the steps in making creamy, fragrant butter chicken.

Ingredients

  • 1 sachet Hart & Soul Butter Chicken Recipe Base
  • 375g chicken breast, diced
  • 150ml pouring cream
  • 70g tomato paste
  • 1 small brown onion, diced
  • 500ml water
  • 1 fresh coriander (to serve, optional)

Steps

  1. In a large frypan, heat 1 tbsp of butter (or oil alternative) over a medium heat.
  2. Add chicken to pan and fry until golden brown.
  3. Add the onion and fry for a further 3-4 minutes.
  4. Add Recipe Base, pouring cream, water and tomato paste into pan. Stir to combine and simmer gently for 10 minutes.
  5. Remove from heat, divide into bowls and garnish with fresh coriander. Serve with rice.
